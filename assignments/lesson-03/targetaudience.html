Target Audience

The target audience is local Idaho residents, as well as tourists visiting Idaho for various reasons. Typical site visitors

Persona 1:

Martha: Wife, homemaker, mother of three, mid-30’s.
Martha needs regular weather updates to help determine how to prepare her kids for school. She also needs to know weekend weather for family plans.

Persona 2:

James: Single, late 20’s, lives alone. James lives in the suburbs and uses public transportation to get to work downtown every day. His commute also involves a bit of walking. He needs to know weather for how to dress, needs to know full day weather for company meetings and dinners.

Persona 3:

Jane: Mid-30’s professional. Jane is in sales, and spends almost every afternoon meeting with clients. She spends a lot of time traveling to various metro areas, so she needs to know about weather not just locally, but across the country to anticipate travel delays, needs, etc.

Scenario 1:

Martha has a playdate planned for her youngest child. She woke up to cloudy skies, and wants to see what the weather will be like for the day. She's ok with clouds, but wants to make sure it will be warm enough for the young kids, and wants to be prepared if any rain is coming.

Scenario 2:

Josh and his friends have an outdoors weekend planned - hiking, fishing, camping, etc. James wants to make sure there is no risk of severe weather, and needs to know what to pack for the weekend.

Scenario 3:

Jane has a very important business trip this week. She wants to make sure the weather will not cause any delays to her flights, and needs to pack appropriately.

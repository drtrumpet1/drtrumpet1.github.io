Color Scheme

Color Scheme: Main colors are #4ABDAC (Fresh), #FC4A1A (Vermillion), #F7B733 (Sunshine), and #DFDCE3 (Clean). Main background is Fresh, with white text. Header will be in Sunshine, bordered by Vermillion. Links in header will be shaded with #FD8666, a lighter shade of Sunshine.
